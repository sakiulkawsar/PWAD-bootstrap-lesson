Everyone needs a place to stay. So, renting a flat or buying a home is the most common thing to notice in our day to day life. City Real Estate, a free real estate marketplace website template is ready for those potential customers. A unique, powerful & SEO optimized free website template with tons of modern features.

About City Real Estate

The free real estate marketplace website template is filled with necessary features and technologies. All begin with the sticky navigation bar which has a search option and social media icons embedded inside. You never know when your potential customers will feel the necessity to contact you. Keeping that in mind, a quick appointment form is built-in right at the beginning. It has some amazing functionality and UI with dropdown options. Parallax effect enabled full-screen background image section runs a slider and the image changes with a smooth transition effect.

This multi-page template includes other essential pages like service page, about or gallery page. What makes it a genuine free real estate marketplace website template is its properties page. This page is solely built for this template. It includes sections like property details and served customers. Furthermore, this template is built on the excellence of Twitter Bootstrap framework. It ensures down to single pixel responsiveness. It supports all the modern browsers and is retina ready.

Features like back to top button, animated preloader, on-hover animation make this free bootstrap template one of a kind. It can compete with any premium real estate templates out there. Finally, a detailed and informative footer section gives this template a finishing touch. This footer section includes a short company bio, important links, contact details and social media icons.
